After four jam-packed days filled to the brim with debate, PLISMUN20 has concluded. In the wake of the conference, you can look back on the daily newspapers, as well as the photos taken throughout the conference. Thanks to all those that came and we hope to see you soon!

About us

We present the Model United Nations conference hosted by Park Lane International School!

Located in the heart of the historic Prague in the Czech Republic, our student-led team strives to make the conference as memorable and enjoyable as possible while engaging in high-yielding debates.

For PLISMUN20, the theme of the conference will be Securing Justice
