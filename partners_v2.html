Why are we looking for partners?

We are looking for partners who could help us organise an international Model United Nations conference for high school students. This conference will be taking place early 2020 in the historical heart of Europe – Prague.

Finance

We are looking for partners who would be able to help us with the financial side of the conference. We are welcome to any sponsorships. PLISMUN is a nonprofit organisation; any money received will be used solely for organisational purposes.

Services

An important part of the conference is food and accomodation. Therefore, we are looking for somebody who would be able to assist us with that. This help can include meals, beverages, accomodation, venues and all other goods and services that concern the delegates.

Materials

We are looking for partners who would be able to help us with supplying utilities as well as printing services for documents and other materials.

Why choose PLISMUN20?

  •       Due to our experience and a target audience of a similar age, we can ensure the success of this conference with confidence.

  •       PLISMUN is a wholely student-led conference with the steering team consisting of dedicated and passionate people.

  •       PLISMUN'19 resulted in substantial interest from international and domestic schools alike. We are expecting an even larger demand this year!

  •       We represent our school, Park Lane IS, and so we wish to and surpass the previous year’s expectations

  •       We encourage other young people to become critical thinkers and global learners, values that we cherish and seek to share with others
